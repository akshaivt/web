ABOUT US

MEA Engineering College, the first NAAC accredited self-financing engineering institution in Malappuram district of Kerala, India. The college is located near Perinthalmanna on the Perinthalmanna-Melattur road. The college is accessible by road, rail and air. The college is governed by the MEA sponsored by the “Samastha Kerala Jammiyyathul Ulama”, a society instituted by intellectuals of the Muslim community. Janab Panakkad Sayed Hyderali Shihab Thangal is its head. The institution is affiliated to the APJ Abdul Kalam Technological University and approved by the A I C T E.
